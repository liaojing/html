Short Bio


I am currently a researcher in Visual Computing Group at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). Before I joined MSRA in Dec. 2015, I received my dual Ph.D. degrees from Zhejiang University and Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of  Prof. Jinhui Yu and Prof. Pedro V. Sander.

Research Interests

  • Computer Graphics

  • Image & video processing

  • Non-photorealistic rendering

  • Animation
